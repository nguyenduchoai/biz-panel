## ğŸ¯ Design Philosophy

### Vision
> **"The elegance of Vercel meets the power of AWS Console"**

### Principles
1. **Clarity over Complexity** - Every action should be obvious
2. **Dark-first** - Server admins work at night
3. **Information Density** - Show more, scroll less
4. **Instant Feedback** - Real-time everything
5. **Progressive Disclosure** - Simple by default, powerful when needed
